How to Use These Sites (Simple Formula)

1) Get structure

Use The Knot for timelines, budgets, and planning flow.

2) Add local truth

Use PartyCharleston.com for destination-specific party tips and “what actually works.”

3) Validate

Cross-check with TripAdvisor + Reddit to avoid overrated or outdated picks.

Pro tip: Build your itinerary around 2 “anchor” activities (like a beach day + nightlife crawl), then fill in the gaps with easy wins: brunch, rooftop drinks, and one late-night dessert stop.
